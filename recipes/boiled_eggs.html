How to Make Boiled Eggs

Boiled eggs is one of those dishes that the whole family can enjoy. Below you'll find a detailed series of instructions for making the best boiled eggs out there.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Water

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Place eggs in water and pot
  3. Heat until boiling, then take off heat with lid on
  4. Take out eggs after 8 minutes
