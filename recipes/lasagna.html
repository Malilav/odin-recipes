How to Make Lasagna

Lasagna is one of those dishes that the whole family can enjoy. Below you'll find a detailed series of instructions for making the best lasagna out there.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Sauce
  • Sausage
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Cook sausage and ground beef in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned
  3. Heat tomato sauce
  4. Boil lasagna pasta
  5. Combine ricotta cheese with egg
  6. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F
  7. Layer the ingredients
  8. Place in oven and cook for 50 minutes
